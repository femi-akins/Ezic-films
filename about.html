About Us

Ezicfilmworks is an independent film production company, established in 2013. We produce moving images, genuinely and passionately directed to tell stories that touch and connect people.
By providing a conducive atmosphere for stories that share our vision, we have managed to build a one-of-a kind slate, these ranges from movies to a documentary about people and brands that communicates history, heritage, values and beliefs, strongly influenced by our cultural roots while a foothold in African Nigeria represents our curiosity and ambition and enables us to aim higher and bolder.
